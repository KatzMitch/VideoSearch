How to Use VideoSearch:

to search for a video follow these 4 simple steps

1. click on the home tab in the top right corner of this page

2. select browse and pick a video file from your computer

3. pick a threshold which determines how close of a match you want your results to have to the video you give us

4. wait for your results witch will load as they are found
